About Me


My name is Hassan Hamdani, and I am a university student with a passion for filmmaking. I believe that with the advancements in technology, the capabilities of cellphones in shooting great films are increasing every day. That's why I decided to create this video archive, to showcase how we can create professional-looking films with just a phone.

Gone are the days when making a film required expensive equipment. With the right techniques and editing tools, anyone can create a beautiful film with just their cellphone. My aim with this video archive is to inspire others to embrace this new way of filmmaking and to show that a phone can be a great substitute in the cinema field.

I hope you enjoy watching the videos and that they inspire you to start creating your own films. Thank you for visiting my page!
